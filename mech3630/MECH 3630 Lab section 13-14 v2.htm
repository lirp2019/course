Emergency situation:

If any student has emergency situation or sick leave, please find any other group member to change

the time schedule with each other. And remember to send an email to TA at least one day before

related Lab session begin. Or there will be an absent record.

 

If it is difficult to change with other group member, the student could also attend other group lab session

as the sixth member only after getting the agreement of that group members. And also remember to

send an email to TA one day before the related Lab session.

 

 

Lab Material:

Lab 1                          Part1 - Three-wire balanced system C star supply with star and delta loads  

                                    Part2 - Three-wire balanced system to unbalanced star load

 

Lab 2                          Auto transformers and current transformers

 

*please download and prepare related manual before lab session

 

Lab Report:

After finish Lab session, each student needs to finish lab report individually.

The lab manual should be submitted as lab report.

(Write down your student ID, full name and group number.)

 

Lab 1                          Write down experimental data on lab 1 manual and finish Analysis and Discussion on extra blank papers. Submit the lab manual and extra paper as lab 1 report.

 

Lab 2                          Write down experimental data on lab 2 manual. After calculation, submit it as lab2 report.

 

 

*The lab report should be submitted into the mail box (with a note written MECH 3630) opposite to the    department office.

  The Due Time is 17:00, 16 May, late submission will be ignored.

  The grading result will be noticed during Spring Term Examinations week, if there is any problem, please contact TA (lirp@ust.hk).
